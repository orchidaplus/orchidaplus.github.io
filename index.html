Not a diagnosis. Orchid A+'s Health Check and Risk Rewind estimate relative risk from a small set of factors and do not know your medical history, medications, or family history. They are educational tools, not medical devices, and do not replace a conversation with a doctor. If you or someone else is experiencing stroke symptoms — sudden numbness, confusion, trouble speaking or seeing, severe headache, loss of balance — call emergency services immediately.
